Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Naija Digest Online ("we", "our", or "us") operates the website naijadigest.ng. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you visit our site, in compliance with the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) and applicable international standards.

1. What We Do

Naija Digest Online is a news aggregator. We automatically collect publicly available headlines and article links from Nigerian and international media outlets and display them in one place. We do not reproduce full articles — all headlines link directly to their original sources.

2. Information We Collect

We collect very little personal information:

  • No user accounts: We do not require registration or login.
  • Advertiser email addresses: If you use our Advertise with Us page, we collect your email address solely to process your payment and deliver your ad placement. We do not sell or share this with third parties.
  • Automatically collected data: Like all websites, our hosting provider may log standard server data such as your IP address, browser type, and pages visited. This data is used only for security and performance monitoring.

3. Cookies

We use cookies primarily through third-party services embedded on this site:

  • Google AdSense: We display ads served by Google AdSense. Google uses cookies to show you relevant ads based on your browsing history. You can opt out of personalised ads by visiting google.com/settings/ads or by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
  • Google Fonts: We load fonts from Google Fonts, which may set cookies for performance tracking.
  • Paystack: Our advertise page uses Paystack for payment processing. Paystack may set cookies to prevent fraud and process transactions securely. See Paystack's Privacy Policy.

By continuing to use this site, you consent to the use of these cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time, though this may affect site functionality.

4. Third-Party Links

All headlines on Naija Digest Online link to external websites we do not control. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those third-party sites. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of any external site you visit.

5. Advertising

This site displays advertisements through two channels:

  • Google AdSense: Automatically placed ads personalised by Google based on your interests and browsing behaviour.
  • Direct advertising: Banner ads purchased directly from us by advertisers. These are static image ads and do not track you.

Advertising revenue helps keep this site free and updated. We do not share your personal data with advertisers.

6. Data Retention

Advertiser email addresses collected during payment are retained for up to 12 months for accounting and dispute resolution purposes, then deleted. We do not store any other personal data.

7. Your Rights

Under the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) and applicable law, you have the right to:

  • Request access to any personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent to data processing at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the National Information Technology Development Agency (NITDA)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address below.

8. Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
